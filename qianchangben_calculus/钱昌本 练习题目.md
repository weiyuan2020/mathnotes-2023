$$

\quad (a,b = \text{const.}, |a| \neq 1)
$$
试问 ${ f(x) }$ 是否有奇偶性？

${ t = \frac{1}{x} }$ 
$$
af\left( \frac{1}{t} \right) + f(t) = bt
$$
两式相加
$$
(a+1) \left( f(x) + f\left( \frac{1}{x} \right) \right) = b\left( x + \frac{1}{x} \right)
$$
$$
f(x) + f\left( \frac{1}{x} \right) = \frac{b}{a+1}\left( x + \frac{1}{x} \right)

$$

$$
易见 ${ f(-x) = -f(x) }$ ${ f(x) }$ 为奇函数
