第一类曲面积分 ${ \mathrm{d}S }$ 
第二类曲面积分 ${ \mathrm{d}x \mathrm{d}y }$ 

曲面方程一般形式
$$
z = z(x,y) \implies f(x,y,z) = 0
$$

全微分 
$$
f_{1}dx + f_{2}dy + f_{3}dz = 0
$$
$$
\frac{f_{1}}{dydz}+
\frac{f_{2}}{dxdz}+
\frac{f_{3}}{dxdy} = 0
$$
$$
\frac{dydz}{f_{1}} =
\frac{dxdz}{f_{2}} =
\frac{dxdy}{f_{3}} = 
\frac{ dS }{ \sqrt{ f_{1}^{2}+f_{2}^{2}+f_{3}^{2} } } 
$$
